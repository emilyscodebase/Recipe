Recipe: Spaghetti Jollof

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti – 500g
  • Tomatoes – 5 medium
  • Red bell peppers – 2
  • Onions – 2
  • Garlic – 2 cloves
  • Vegetable oil – 3 tbsp
  • Salt – to taste
  • Seasoning cubes – 2
  • Water – as needed

Instructions

  1. Boil water and cook spaghetti until slightly tender. Drain and set aside.
  2. Blend tomatoes, peppers, onions, and garlic into a smooth paste.
  3. Heat oil in a pot and fry the blended mix for 10 minutes.
  4. Add seasoning and salt, then stir well.
  5. Add the cooked spaghetti into the sauce and mix until coated.
  6. Let it cook on low heat for another 5 minutes.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!

Notes

You can add chicken, fish, or vegetables to make it richer.
